## 🎨 Color Scheme

### New Palette
- **Primary:** Violet-600 (buttons, active states)
- **Success:** Green (refill/cancel indicators)
- **Warning:** Orange (errors, logs)
- **Info:** Blue (categories, stats)
- **Neutral:** Slate (backgrounds, text)

### Gradients Used
- Stat cards: Individual gradient backgrounds
- Pagination: Gradient from slate-50 to white
- Loading: Gradient from slate-50 to white
- Table header: Gradient from slate-100 to slate-50
- Active page: Violet-600 to violet-700
